About

Who is Dana Ter?

With a decade of journalism experience, I write guidebooks, articles and content for in-flight magazines, travel booking sites, hotels and tourism bureaus. I've worked with EVA Air, Singapore Airlines, Scoot, Philippine Airlines, Hong Kong Airlines, SilkAir, ebookers (Expedia), Marriott Hotels, DestinAsian, and Taiwan's Tourism Bureau. And I've filed stories from all over Asia, Europe, Australia, North America and South America, and interviewed politicians, surfers, fortune tellers, farmers, celebrity chefs, cat breeders and fashion designers.

I wrote the Singapore content for Marriott Hotels' website, including weekend itineraries and neighborhood guides. From 2018-2021, I was the Taipei Correspondent for Louis Vuitton City Guides, published by BETC Paris. I wrote the second edition print guide published in English and French and sold in Louis Vuitton stores worldwide. I also wrote and edited the Taipei digital guide on iPhone app which covers hotels, restaurants, nightlife, art and fashion. In 2021, I authored a guidebook about Taiwan's cultural and nature attractions, dining and festivals for Taiwan's Tourism Bureau in collaboration with CNBC Singapore. From 2014-2017, I was staff reporter and editor for the Features section of the English-language daily newspaper, the Taipei Times, where I launched a column about Taiwan's craft beer, and wrote a weekly column about art exhibitions. Before that, I was an entertainment news columnist for Mic in New York City.

I have a Dual Master's degree in International and World History from Columbia University and the London School of Economics and am a licensed PADI Open Water Diver. On my mom's side, I'm descended from generations of Peranakan seafarers and explorers from Penang and Singapore, while my dad's family is from Kuala Lumpur. Born in Malaysia, I have Chinese, Malay and Thai ancestry and spent most of my teenage years in Taiwan. I speak, read and write Mandarin and some Spanish, Malay and Penang Hokkien. Growing up, I attended American schools in Hong Kong, Jakarta, Singapore, Kuala Lumpur and Taipei and have also lived in Melbourne, Bangkok, Seoul, NYC, London, Stockholm and Sicily (Catania). Since mid-2021, I've been based in Singapore, Malaysia and Europe. I’m writing a fiction novel inspired by the year I spent the pandemic on a remote subtropical beach in southern Taiwan with a small community of local surfers and five dogs.
