Who is Dana Ter?

I grew up as a Third Culture Kid in Malaysia, Indonesia, Singapore, Thailand, Hong Kong and Taiwan, and was educated in Australia, the US and the UK. My family currently lives in South Korea and I work as a journalist in Taipei, Taiwan. Before that, I was a freelance writer based in New York City's Upper West Side. If I had to list all the countries I've traveled to, it wouldn't fit on this page.

While I do enjoy my share of steamed dumplings and beef noodles now, I still crave cream cheese bagels and pastrami sandwiches with pickles. When I'm not tapping away on my laptop, I'm usually doodling in my sketchbook, looking for a good surf beach, plotting my next international (mis)adventure, or trying to tame my fiery side by practicing yoga. I have a Dual MA/MSc in International and World History from Columbia University and the London School of Economics.

2015-02-25, By Dana Ter
