I grew up as a Third Culture Kid in Malaysia, Indonesia, Singapore, Thailand, Hong Kong and Taiwan, and was educated in Australia, the US and the UK.

My family currently lives in South Korea and I work as a journalist in Taipei, Taiwan. Before that, I was a freelance writer based in New York City's Upper West Side.

When I'm not tapping away on my laptop, I'm usually doodling in my sketchbook, wandering around in search of a deserted beach, plotting my next international (mis)adventure, or trying to tame my fiery side by practicing yoga.

I have a Dual MA/MSc in International and World History from Columbia University and the London School of Economics.
